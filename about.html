About Us

"Education for All" is a nonprofit organization founded by two high school students, Brook and Netanya. They recognized a fundamental flaw in our country's educational system: the learning methodology. This issue was starkly illustrated by the national exam scores, which boasted a mere 3% passing rate. In response, Brook and Netanya took initiative on a modest scale by creating a platform where knowledge could be freely shared among our populace — Education4All!

Mission

Our mission is to dismantle the barriers to education that many Ethiopian students face. We specialize in English and math, providing a robust foundation for future academic and professional success. Our Telegram channel serves as a conduit for knowledge, delivering impactful educational content directly to students.

Content

Each piece of content we create is a stepping stone towards academic excellence. Our Telegram channel is a treasure trove of knowledge, featuring in-depth tutorials, practice problems, and interactive sessions in English and math. Meanwhile, our YouTube channel is a linguistic launchpad, offering podcasts that make mastering German and French not just possible, but enjoyable.

Impact

Education for All’ has become synonymous with academic improvement and success. Our initiative has been a catalyst for change, with students sharing stories of triumph over exams and newfound love for learning. These stories are not just our pride—they are our driving force.

Vision

Our vision is a world where every student in Ethiopia has the resources to excel academically and the skills to thrive in a globalized world. We aim to be the cornerstone of a movement that heralds an era of educational renaissance in Ethiopia.

Call to Action

Your journey towards academic prowess starts here. Dive into our Telegram channel, immerse yourself in our language podcasts on YouTube, and join a community that’s reshaping the landscape of education in Ethiopia. Together, we can turn the page to a brighter chapter.
